Baked Cod

Lemon herb butter, panko topping

Prep: Cook: Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 cod fillets (6 oz each)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp butter, melted
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 2 tbsp parsley
  • 1 tbsp dill
  • ¼ cup panko
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Pat cod dry, season.
  2. Mix butter, 2 tbsp oil, garlic, lemon zest, herbs.
  3. Place cod in baking dish. Spoon herb butter over.
  4. Mix panko with remaining oil. Sprinkle over fish.
  5. Bake until fish flakes and topping is golden, 15-18 minutes.
  6. Squeeze lemon juice over. Serve.

Notes

  • Cod's mild flavor pairs with bold toppings.
  • Don't overcook - should just flake.
