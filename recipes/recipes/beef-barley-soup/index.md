# Beef Barley Soup

Hearty, beefy, cold-weather comfort

Serves: 6-8

## Ingredients

- 1½ lbs beef chuck, cubed
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 2 carrots, chopped
- 2 celery ribs, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 8 cups beef broth
- 2 cups water
- ¾ cup pearl barley
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 tsp thyme
- 2 tbsp parsley

## Steps

1. Brown beef in batches in oil, about 5 minutes per batch.
2. Cook onion, carrots, celery until softened, 8 minutes.
3. Add garlic and tomato paste, cook 1 minute.
4. Add broth, water, barley, bay leaves, thyme. Return beef.
5. Simmer covered until beef and barley are tender, 1¼ hours.
6. Remove bay leaves, stir in parsley. Season to taste.

## Notes

- Don't skip browning the beef - builds the flavor base.
- Add more broth when reheating - barley absorbs liquid.
