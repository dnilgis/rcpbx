# Chicken Stir Fry

Tender chicken, crisp vegetables, savory sauce

Serves: 4

## Ingredients

- 1½ lbs chicken breast, sliced thin
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 1 tbsp Shaoxing wine
- 3 tbsp vegetable oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, minced
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup broccoli
- ½ cup chicken broth
- 2 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil

## Steps

1. Toss chicken with 1 tbsp soy sauce, cornstarch, wine. Marinate 15 minutes.
2. Mix broth, remaining soy sauce, oyster sauce.
3. Heat wok over high until smoking. Add oil.
4. Sear chicken without stirring 1 minute, then stir-fry until just cooked. Transfer out.
5. Stir-fry garlic and ginger 30 seconds. Add vegetables, stir-fry 2 minutes.
6. Return chicken, add sauce. Cook until thickened.
7. Drizzle with sesame oil. Serve over rice.

## Notes

- Velveting (cornstarch marinade) keeps chicken tender.
- Wok must be ripping hot.
- Cook in batches to avoid steaming.
