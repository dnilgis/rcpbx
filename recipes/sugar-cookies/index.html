Sugar Cookies

Crisp edges, soft center, perfect for decorating

Time2 hrs + chilling
Serves24-36

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • ½ tsp almond extract (optional)
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • Royal icing for decorating

Steps

  1. Beat butter and sugar until fluffy, 3 minutes.
  2. Add egg and extracts.
  3. Whisk flour, baking powder, salt. Add gradually.
  4. Divide dough, flatten, wrap, chill 2 hours.
  5. Roll to ¼-inch thick. Cut shapes.
  6. Place on parchment sheets. Chill 15 minutes.
  7. Bake at 350°F for 8-10 minutes until edges just start to color.
  8. Cool completely before decorating.

Notes

Chilling prevents spreading.

Don't overbake - they firm as they cool.

Almond extract is the secret.
