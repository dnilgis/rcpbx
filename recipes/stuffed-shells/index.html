Stuffed Shells

Ricotta-filled pasta in marinara

Time 1¼ hrs
Serves 8

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 oz) box jumbo shells
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (24 oz) jar marinara
  • 32 oz ricotta
  • 2 cups mozzarella, shredded
  • ½ cup Parmesan
  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ cup parsley
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook shells until just al dente. Cool.
  2. Brown beef with onion. Stir in half the marinara.
  3. Mix ricotta, 1 cup mozzarella, Parmesan, eggs, parsley, Italian seasoning.
  4. Spread remaining marinara in 9x13 dish.
  5. Fill shells with ricotta, place in dish. Top with meat sauce and remaining mozzarella.
  6. Cover, bake at 350°F for 40 minutes. Uncover, bake 10 more minutes.

Notes

Undercook shells so they don't tear.

Piping bag makes filling faster.
