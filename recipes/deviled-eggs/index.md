# Deviled Eggs

The party appetizer that disappears first. Every. Single. Time.

Prep: 20 min · Cook: 12 min · Makes: 24 halves

## Ingredients

- 12 large eggs
- 1/3 cup mayonnaise
- 1 Tbsp Dijon mustard
- 1 Tbsp yellow mustard
- 1 Tbsp white vinegar or pickle juice
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp black pepper
- Paprika for garnish
- Fresh chives for garnish

## Steps

1. Place eggs in a single layer in a saucepan. Cover with cold water by 1 inch.
2. Bring to a rolling boil over high heat. Remove from heat, cover, and let sit 12 minutes.
3. Transfer eggs to an ice bath for 5 minutes. Peel under cool running water.
4. Slice eggs in half lengthwise. Gently remove yolks to a bowl.
5. Mash yolks with a fork. Add mayo, both mustards, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Mix until smooth.
6. Pipe or spoon filling into egg white halves.
7. Sprinkle with paprika and chives. Refrigerate until serving.

## Notes

- Easy peel: Older eggs (1-2 weeks old) peel much easier than fresh eggs.
- Steam method: Steam eggs 12 minutes instead of boiling for easier peeling.
- Pipe it: Use a zip-lock bag with the corner cut off for a cleaner look.
- Make ahead: Fill eggs up to 24 hours ahead. Cover tightly with plastic wrap.
- Variations: Add crumbled bacon, relish, hot sauce, or everything bagel seasoning.
