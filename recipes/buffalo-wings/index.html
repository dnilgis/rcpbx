Buffalo Wings

Crispy oven-baked, classic hot sauce

Time1 hr + overnight
Serves4

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs chicken wings, split
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • ½ cup Frank's RedHot
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar
  • ¼ tsp Worcestershire
  • Blue cheese dressing and celery

Steps

  1. Pat wings dry. Toss with baking powder and salt.
  2. Arrange on wire rack over sheet. Refrigerate uncovered overnight.
  3. Bake at 450°F for 20 minutes. Flip, bake 20-30 more until crispy.
  4. Melt butter, whisk in hot sauce, vinegar, Worcestershire.
  5. Toss hot wings in sauce.
  6. Serve with blue cheese and celery.

Notes

Baking powder + overnight = crispy without frying.

Don't skip overnight rest.

Frank's is classic; adjust heat with more/less sauce.
