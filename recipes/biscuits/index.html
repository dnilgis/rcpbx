Buttermilk Biscuits

Tall, flaky, butter-brushed

Time30 min
Serves8-10

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 6 tbsp cold butter, cubed
  • ¾ cup cold buttermilk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Whisk flour, baking powder, baking soda, sugar, salt.
  3. Cut in cold butter until pea-sized pieces remain.
  4. Add buttermilk. Stir until just combined.
  5. Pat to 1-inch thick. Fold in half. Repeat twice.
  6. Pat to 1-inch thick. Cut with 2.5-inch cutter. Don't twist.
  7. Place on sheet, sides touching.
  8. Bake until golden, 15-18 minutes.

Notes

Cold butter is essential.

Don't twist cutter - seals edges.

Biscuits touching rise higher.
