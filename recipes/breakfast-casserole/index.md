# Breakfast Casserole

Make-ahead, feeds a crowd

Serves: 8-10

## Ingredients

- 1 lb breakfast sausage
- 6 slices bread, cubed
- 2 cups cheddar, shredded
- 6 eggs
- 2 cups milk
- 1 tsp dry mustard
- ½ tsp salt

## Steps

1. Brown sausage, breaking into crumbles. Drain.
2. Layer bread, sausage, cheese in greased 9x13 dish.
3. Whisk eggs, milk, mustard, salt. Pour over casserole.
4. Cover, refrigerate overnight.
5. Bake uncovered at 350°F for 45-50 minutes until set and golden.
6. Rest 10 minutes.

## Notes

- Overnight rest lets bread absorb custard.
- Great for holiday mornings.
