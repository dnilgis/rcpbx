# Pan-Seared Steak

Restaurant-quality crust, butter-basted

Serves: 2

## Ingredients

- 2 ribeye or NY strip steaks (1-1.5 inches)
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 3 tbsp butter
- 4 cloves garlic, smashed
- 4 sprigs thyme
- Flaky salt and coarse pepper

## Steps

1. Remove steaks from fridge 30-60 minutes before. Pat very dry. Season generously.
2. Heat oil in cast iron over high until smoking.
3. Lay steaks in pan. Sear 3-4 minutes until deep crust forms.
4. Flip. Add butter, garlic, herbs. Tilt pan and baste continuously 2-3 minutes.
5. Check temp: 120°F rare, 130°F medium-rare, 140°F medium.
6. Rest 5-10 minutes. Slice against grain.

## Notes

- Room temp steak cooks evenly.
- Dry surface = better crust.
- Don't touch while searing.
- Resting redistributes juices.
