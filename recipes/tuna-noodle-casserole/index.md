# Tuna Noodle Casserole

Creamy, comforting, budget-friendly classic.

Prep: 15 min · Cook: 30 min · Serves: 6

## Ingredients

- 12 oz egg noodles
- 4 tbsp butter
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 stalks celery, diced
- ¼ cup flour
- 2 cups milk
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 2 cans 5oz tuna, drained
- 1 cup frozen peas
- 1 cup shredded cheddar
- ½ cup breadcrumbs
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Cook noodles according to package, drain.
2. Melt butter in large oven-safe skillet. Sauté onion and celery until soft.
3. Whisk in flour, cook 1 minute.
4. Gradually whisk in milk and broth. Simmer until thickened.
5. Remove from heat. Stir in tuna, peas, half the cheese, noodles. Season.
6. Top with remaining cheese and breadcrumbs.
7. Bake 20-25 minutes until bubbly and golden.

## Notes

- Upgrade the tuna: Oil-packed has better flavor than water-packed.
- Add mushrooms: Sauté with the onion for extra depth.
- Crispy top: Broil last 2 minutes for extra crunch.
