Ground Beef Tacos

Well-seasoned, classic weeknight dinner

Time25 min
Serves4-6

Ingredients

  • 1½ lbs ground beef (80/20)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 small onion, minced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • ½ tsp oregano
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp cider vinegar
  • Corn tortillas
  • Onion, cilantro, salsa, lime

Steps

  1. Cook onion in oil 3 minutes. Add beef, break into pieces. Brown 5-7 minutes.
  2. Add garlic, chili powder, cumin, oregano. Cook 1 minute.
  3. Add broth and vinegar. Simmer until mostly absorbed, 5 minutes.
  4. Season with salt.
  5. Warm tortillas. Serve with onion, cilantro, salsa, lime.

Notes

80/20 has enough fat for juicy tacos.

Browning before spices develops flavor.

Vinegar adds brightness.
