Crab Cakes

Minimal filler, maximum crab

Time45 min
Serves4

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lump crabmeat
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire
  • 1 tbsp Old Bay
  • 2 tbsp parsley
  • ½ cup panko
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Lemon and tartar sauce

Steps

  1. Pick through crabmeat for shells, keeping lumps intact.
  2. Whisk mayo, egg yolk, mustard, Worcestershire, Old Bay, parsley.
  3. Gently fold in crabmeat and ¼ cup panko. Don't break up lumps.
  4. Form into 4 patties. Coat with remaining panko.
  5. Refrigerate 30 minutes.
  6. Cook in butter over medium until golden, 3-4 minutes per side.
  7. Serve with lemon and tartar sauce.

Notes

Best crabmeat = best cakes.

Handle gently to preserve lumps.

Chilling firms them up.
