# Rice Pilaf

Buttery, toasted, fluffy

Serves: 4-6

## Ingredients

- 1½ cups long-grain white rice
- 2¼ cups chicken broth
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 small onion, minced
- ½ tsp salt

## Steps

1. Rinse rice until water runs clear. Drain.
2. Melt butter over medium. Cook onion 3 minutes.
3. Add rice. Stir to coat. Toast until edges turn translucent, 2-3 minutes.
4. Add broth and salt. Bring to boil.
5. Cover, reduce to low. Simmer 18 minutes.
6. Remove from heat. Let sit 5 minutes covered.
7. Fluff with fork.

## Notes

- Rinsing removes starch for fluffy rice.
- Toasting adds nutty flavor.
- Don't lift lid while cooking.
