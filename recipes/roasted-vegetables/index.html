Roasted Vegetables

Caramelized, tender, versatile

Prep: Cook: Serves: 4-6

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs mixed vegetables (carrots, broccoli, cauliflower, Brussels sprouts)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme or rosemary
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Cut vegetables into similar sizes.
  3. Toss with oil, garlic, herbs, salt, pepper.
  4. Spread in single layer on baking sheet. Don't crowd.
  5. Roast 20 minutes. Stir.
  6. Continue roasting until browned and tender, 15-25 more minutes.

Notes

  • Don't crowd the pan.
  • Use two pans if needed.
  • High heat creates caramelization.
