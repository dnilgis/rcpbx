Glazed Ham

Holiday centerpiece, sweet and sticky

Time2½-3 hrs
Serves12-15

Ingredients

  • 1 bone-in spiral-sliced ham (8-10 lbs)
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup honey
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • ¼ tsp cloves
  • ¼ tsp cinnamon

Steps

  1. Place ham cut-side down in roasting pan.
  2. Cover tightly with foil. Bake at 250°F until 100°F internal, about 1.5-2 hours.
  3. Mix glaze ingredients.
  4. Remove foil. Increase oven to 350°F.
  5. Brush with half the glaze. Bake 15 minutes.
  6. Brush with remaining glaze. Bake until 120°F and caramelized, 15-20 minutes.
  7. Rest 15 minutes.

Notes

Spiral ham is already cooked - just heating and glazing.

Cut-side down keeps meat moist.
