# Beef and Broccoli

Chinese takeout classic, better at home

Serves: 4

## Ingredients

- 1 lb flank steak, sliced thin against grain
- 2 tbsp soy sauce, divided
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 1 tbsp Shaoxing wine
- 3 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 lb broccoli florets
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, minced
- ½ cup beef broth
- 2 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil

## Steps

1. Toss beef with 1 tbsp soy sauce, cornstarch, wine. Marinate 15 minutes.
2. Mix broth, remaining soy sauce, oyster sauce.
3. Heat wok until smoking. Add 2 tbsp oil. Sear beef 1 minute, stir-fry 1-2 minutes. Transfer out.
4. Add remaining oil. Stir-fry broccoli 2 minutes. Add 2 tbsp water, cover, steam 1 minute.
5. Add garlic and ginger, 30 seconds. Add sauce and beef. Toss until thickened.
6. Drizzle with sesame oil. Serve over rice.

## Notes

- Velveting keeps beef tender.
- Slice against grain for tender pieces.
