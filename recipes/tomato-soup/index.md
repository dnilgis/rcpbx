# Tomato Soup

Velvety smooth, deeply tomatoey. Grilled cheese's best friend.

Prep: 10 min · Cook: 30 min · Serves: 6

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 cans 28oz whole peeled tomatoes
- 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
- 1 tsp sugar
- ½ cup heavy cream
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh basil for garnish

## Steps

1. Melt butter in large pot over medium heat.
2. Sauté onion until soft, about 5 minutes. Add garlic, cook 1 minute.
3. Add tomatoes with juices, broth, and sugar. Bring to simmer.
4. Cook 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
5. Blend until smooth with immersion blender (or in batches in regular blender).
6. Stir in cream. Season with salt and pepper.
7. Serve hot, garnished with basil.

## Notes

- San Marzano tomatoes: Worth the splurge for best flavor.
- Don't boil after adding cream: It can curdle.
- Make it chunky: Blend only half for more texture.
