Protein Ice Cream

High protein, low calorie, actually tastes good.

Prep: 5 min Cook: 0 min Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 frozen bananas, sliced
  • 1 scoop vanilla or chocolate protein powder
  • 2-3 Tbsp milk of choice
  • 1 Tbsp peanut butter (optional)
  • 1/4 cup frozen berries (optional)
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Freeze ripe bananas (the riper, the sweeter). Slice before freezing for easier blending.
  2. Add frozen banana slices to a food processor or high-powered blender.
  3. Add protein powder and a splash of milk. Pulse several times to break up bananas.
  4. Blend until smooth and creamy, scraping down sides as needed. Add more milk 1 Tbsp at a time if too thick.
  5. Add peanut butter or frozen berries if using. Pulse to combine.
  6. Serve immediately for soft-serve texture, or freeze 30 min for firmer scoop.

Notes

  • Use overripe bananas for best sweetness - the brown spots are your friend.
  • A food processor works better than most blenders for this texture.
  • Add cocoa powder for chocolate version without chocolate protein powder.
