Fresh Salsa

Bright, chunky, restaurant style

Time15 min
Serves2 cups

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ripe tomatoes, diced
  • ¼ cup white onion, minced
  • 1-2 jalapeños, seeded and minced
  • ¼ cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Combine tomatoes, onion, jalapeño, cilantro, garlic in bowl.
  2. Add lime juice and salt. Toss gently.
  3. Let sit 15 minutes for flavors to meld.
  4. Taste and adjust seasoning.
  5. Best served within a few hours.

Notes

Ripe tomatoes make all the difference.

Seed jalapeños for less heat.

Salt draws out tomato juices.
