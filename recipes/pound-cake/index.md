# Pound Cake

Dense, buttery, versatile

Serves: 12

## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter, softened
- 3 cups sugar
- 6 eggs
- 3 cups flour
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 tsp vanilla
- ¼ tsp salt

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 325°F. Grease and flour bundt pan.
2. Beat butter and sugar until very light and fluffy, 5 minutes.
3. Add eggs one at a time, beating after each.
4. Combine flour and salt. Add alternately with sour cream, starting and ending with flour.
5. Stir in vanilla.
6. Pour into pan. Bake 60-75 minutes until toothpick comes out clean.
7. Cool in pan 15 minutes, then turn out.

## Notes

- Creaming butter and sugar is the key to texture.
- Don't open oven door for first 45 minutes.
