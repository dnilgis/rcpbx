Pumpkin Pie

Spiced, custardy, Thanksgiving essential

Time1½ hrs + chilling
Serves8

Ingredients

  • 1 (9-inch) pie crust, blind-baked
  • 1 (15 oz) can pumpkin puree
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ginger
  • ¼ tsp nutmeg
  • ¼ tsp cloves
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Whipped cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Cook pumpkin in saucepan over medium, stirring, until thickened, 5-8 minutes.
  3. Whisk in sugar, spices, salt. Cool slightly.
  4. Whisk in eggs and cream.
  5. Pour into crust. Bake 15 minutes.
  6. Reduce to 350°F. Bake 40-50 more until center is just set.
  7. Cool to room temperature. Chill.
  8. Serve with whipped cream.

Notes

Cooking pumpkin first intensifies flavor.

Center should wobble when done.
