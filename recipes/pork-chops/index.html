Pan-Seared Pork Chops

Golden crust, juicy center, butter-basted

Time25 min
Serves4

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in pork chops (1-inch thick)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 4 sprigs fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Remove chops from fridge 30 minutes before. Pat very dry.
  2. Season generously with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat oil in cast iron over medium-high until smoking.
  4. Sear without moving 4 minutes until deep golden.
  5. Flip. Add butter, garlic, thyme.
  6. Tilt pan and baste 3-4 minutes.
  7. Check temp: 140°F for slightly pink, 145°F for medium.
  8. Rest 5 minutes.

Notes

Thick-cut bone-in stays juicier.

Pork is safe at 145°F.
