# Shepherd's Pie

Savory meat filling topped with mashed potatoes

Serves: 6-8

## Ingredients

- 2 lbs russet potatoes, cubed
- 6 tbsp butter, divided
- ½ cup heavy cream
- 1½ lbs ground beef
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 celery ribs, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 cup beef broth
- 1 cup frozen peas
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire
- 1 tsp thyme

## Steps

1. Boil potatoes until tender. Mash with 4 tbsp butter and cream. Season.
2. Brown beef, breaking into pieces. Drain, transfer out.
3. Cook vegetables in remaining butter until soft, 8 minutes.
4. Add garlic and tomato paste, cook 1 minute.
5. Return beef. Add broth, peas, Worcestershire, thyme. Simmer 10 minutes. Season.
6. Spread mashed potatoes over filling. Rough up surface with fork.
7. Broil until golden, 5-10 minutes.

## Notes

- 'Shepherd's pie' uses lamb; 'cottage pie' uses beef.
- Roughing up potatoes creates crispy peaks.
