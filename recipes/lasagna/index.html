Lasagna

The crowd-pleaser. Beef and sausage, three cheeses, feeds an army.

Prep: 45 min Cook: 2½ hrs Serves: 12

Ingredients

  • Meat Sauce: 1 lb ground beef, 1 lb Italian sausage, 1 onion diced, 4 garlic cloves minced, 28 oz crushed tomatoes, 12 oz tomato paste, 15 oz tomato sauce, ½ cup water, 2 tbsp sugar, 1 tsp fennel seeds, 1 tsp Italian seasoning, 1 tbsp fresh basil chopped, salt and pepper
  • Ricotta Mixture: 30 oz ricotta cheese, 1 egg, ½ cup fresh parsley chopped, ½ tsp salt
  • Assembly: 12 lasagna noodles, 1 lb mozzarella sliced (not shredded), 1 cup parmesan grated

Instructions

  1. Brown beef and sausage in a Dutch oven over medium heat. Drain fat. Add onion, cook 5 min. Add garlic, cook 1 min.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, water, sugar, fennel, Italian seasoning, and basil. Simmer uncovered 1½ hours, stirring occasionally.
  3. Meanwhile, cook noodles according to package. Drain and lay flat on oiled sheet to prevent sticking.
  4. Mix ricotta with egg, parsley, and salt in a bowl.
  5. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  6. Layer in 9x13 pan: 1½ cups sauce, 4 noodles, ⅓ ricotta mixture, ⅓ mozzarella slices. Repeat 2 more times. Top with remaining sauce and parmesan.
  7. Cover with foil. Bake 25 min. Remove foil, bake 25 min more until bubbly.
  8. Rest 15 minutes before cutting (this is crucial for clean slices).

Notes

  • Why 1½ hours for sauce? The long simmer mellows acidity and develops deep flavor. Don't skip it.
  • Sliced vs shredded mozzarella: Sliced melts in distinct creamy layers. Shredded clumps.
  • Make ahead: Assemble, cover, refrigerate overnight. Add 15 min to bake time if cold.
  • Storage: Keeps 5 days refrigerated. Freezes beautifully for 3 months.
