# Green Bean Casserole

Creamy, crunchy, holiday favorite

Serves: 8-10

## Ingredients

- 2 lbs fresh green beans, trimmed
- 8 oz cremini mushrooms, sliced
- 3 tbsp butter
- 3 tbsp flour
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 tsp soy sauce
- 1½ cups fried onions (French's)
- Salt and pepper

## Steps

1. Blanch green beans in salted water 4 minutes. Ice bath. Drain well.
2. Melt butter. Cook mushrooms until browned, 8 minutes. Season.
3. Add flour, cook 1 minute. Whisk in broth and cream.
4. Simmer until thickened, 5 minutes. Add soy sauce.
5. Combine beans and sauce in 9x13 dish.
6. Top with 1 cup fried onions.
7. Bake at 375°F for 25 minutes. Add remaining onions. Bake 5 more minutes.

## Notes

- Fresh beans are so much better than canned.
- Adding onions at end keeps them crispy.
