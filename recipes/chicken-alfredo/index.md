# Chicken Alfredo

Creamy, garlicky, comfort food perfection

Serves: 4

## Ingredients

- 1 lb fettuccine
- 1 lb chicken breasts
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 6 tbsp butter
- 2 cups heavy cream
- 2 cups freshly grated Parmesan
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- ¼ tsp nutmeg
- Salt and pepper
- Fresh parsley

## Steps

1. Season and cook chicken in oil until golden, 6-7 minutes per side. Rest, then slice.
2. Cook pasta until al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water.
3. Melt butter, add garlic, cook 30 seconds. Add cream, simmer 2-3 minutes.
4. Off heat, gradually stir in Parmesan until melted.
5. Add pasta, toss. Add pasta water for silky consistency.
6. Season with nutmeg, salt, pepper. Top with chicken and parsley.

## Notes

- Real Alfredo is just butter, Parmesan, pasta water - cream is American addition.
- Take pot off heat before adding cheese to prevent clumping.
