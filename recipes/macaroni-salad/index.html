Macaroni Salad

Creamy, tangy, picnic classic

Time30 min + chilling
Serves8

Ingredients

  • 1 lb elbow macaroni
  • ¾ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 celery ribs, diced
  • ¼ cup red onion, minced
  • ¼ cup dill pickles, diced
  • 2 tbsp parsley
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook pasta until tender. Rinse with cold water.
  2. Whisk mayo, vinegar, mustard.
  3. Add pasta, celery, onion, pickles, parsley. Toss.
  4. Season generously.
  5. Refrigerate at least 2 hours.
  6. Before serving, stir and adjust. Add more mayo if dry.

Notes

Pasta absorbs dressing - make it wetter than you think.

Tastes better next day.
