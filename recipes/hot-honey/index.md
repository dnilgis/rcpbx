# Hot Honey

Sweet heat that goes on everything. The condiment that defined 2025.

Prep: 5 min · Cook: 10 min · Makes: 1 cup

## Ingredients

- 1 cup honey
- 2-4 Tbsp hot sauce (like Frank's or Cholula)
- 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
- 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (optional, for extra heat)
- 1 Tbsp apple cider vinegar

## Steps

1. Combine honey, hot sauce, red pepper flakes, and cayenne (if using) in a small saucepan.
2. Heat over low heat for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally. Don't let it boil.
3. Remove from heat and stir in apple cider vinegar.
4. Let cool slightly, then taste and adjust heat level.
5. Transfer to a jar. Store at room temperature up to 1 month.

## Notes

- Heat levels: Start with 2 Tbsp hot sauce and add more to taste. You can always add heat, can't take it away.
- Fresno or serrano: For a fresher kick, slice 1-2 fresh chilis and simmer them in the honey instead of hot sauce.
- Best on: Pizza (especially pepperoni), fried chicken, biscuits, bacon, cheese boards, vanilla ice cream.
- Vinegar: Adds depth and cuts the sweetness. Don't skip it.
- No-cook version: Just whisk ingredients together without heating. Flavor won't meld as well but works in a pinch.
