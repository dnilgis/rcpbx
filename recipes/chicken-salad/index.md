# Chicken Salad

Classic, creamy, perfect for sandwiches

Serves: 4-6

## Ingredients

- 1½ lbs chicken breasts
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 2 celery ribs, chopped fine
- 2 tbsp fresh parsley
- 1 shallot, minced
- Salt and pepper

## Steps

1. Cover chicken with salted water. Bring to boil, reduce to simmer. Cook until 160°F, 15-20 minutes.
2. Cool 10 minutes, then shred or chop.
3. Whisk mayo, lemon juice, mustard.
4. Add chicken, celery, parsley, shallot. Toss.
5. Season with salt and pepper. Chill 30 minutes.
6. Serve on bread, in lettuce cups, or with crackers.

## Notes

- Poaching keeps chicken moist.
- Shredded has better texture than cubed.
