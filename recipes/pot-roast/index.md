# Pot Roast

Fork-tender beef, rich gravy

Serves: 6-8

## Ingredients

- 1 boneless beef chuck roast (3-4 lbs)
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 large onion, sliced
- 2 carrots, chunked
- 2 celery ribs, chunked
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup red wine
- 2 cups beef broth
- 1 (14 oz) can diced tomatoes
- 2 bay leaves
- 2 sprigs thyme

## Steps

1. Season roast generously. Brown in oil on all sides, 10-12 minutes. Transfer out.
2. Cook onion, carrots, celery until soft, 8 minutes. Add garlic, 30 seconds.
3. Add wine, scrape up bits. Reduce by half.
4. Add broth, tomatoes, bay leaves, thyme. Return roast.
5. Cover, braise at 300°F until fork-tender, 3-3.5 hours.
6. Slice against grain. Serve with strained sauce and vegetables.

## Notes

- Chuck has best fat marbling.
- Low and slow is essential.
- Let meat rest before slicing.
