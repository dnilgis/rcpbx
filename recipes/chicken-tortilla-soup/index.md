# Chicken Tortilla Soup

Smoky, spicy, with crispy tortilla strips

Serves: 6

## Ingredients

- 8 corn tortillas, cut into strips
- Vegetable oil for frying
- 1 onion, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 jalapeños, minced
- 1 tbsp tomato paste
- 1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes
- 8 cups chicken broth
- 1 lb chicken breasts
- ¼ cup cilantro
- Juice of 2 limes
- Avocado, sour cream, cheese

## Steps

1. Fry half the tortilla strips until crisp. Drain, season with salt.
2. Sauté onion 5 minutes. Add remaining tortilla strips, garlic, jalapeños, tomato paste. Cook 2 minutes.
3. Add tomatoes and broth. Simmer.
4. Add chicken breasts, simmer until cooked, 15-20 minutes.
5. Remove chicken, shred, return to pot.
6. Stir in cilantro and lime juice.
7. Serve topped with fried tortillas, avocado, sour cream, cheese.

## Notes

- Cooking tortillas in soup adds body and flavor.
- Crispy strips must be added right before serving.
