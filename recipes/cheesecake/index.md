# New York Cheesecake

Dense, creamy, classic

Serves: 12

## Ingredients

- Crust: 2 cups graham cracker crumbs, 6 tbsp butter melted, 2 tbsp sugar
- Filling: 32 oz cream cheese softened, 1 cup sugar, 1 tbsp vanilla, ¼ tsp salt, 4 eggs, ⅔ cup sour cream

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 325°F. Wrap springform pan with foil.
2. Mix crust ingredients. Press into pan bottom and 1 inch up sides.
3. Bake crust 10 minutes. Cool.
4. Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add vanilla and salt.
5. Add eggs one at a time. Stir in sour cream.
6. Pour into crust. Place in roasting pan, add hot water halfway up sides.
7. Bake until edges set and center jiggles slightly, 60-70 minutes.
8. Turn off oven, crack door, cool 1 hour in oven.
9. Refrigerate overnight.

## Notes

- Room temp cream cheese prevents lumps.
- Water bath prevents cracking.
- Must chill overnight.
