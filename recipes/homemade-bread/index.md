# Homemade Bread

Crusty outside, soft inside

Makes: 1 loaf

## Ingredients

- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 1½ tsp salt
- ½ tsp instant yeast
- 1½ cups warm water

## Steps

1. Mix flour, salt, yeast. Add water, stir until shaggy dough forms.
2. Cover, let rise at room temp 12-18 hours.
3. Turn dough onto floured surface. Fold edges to center, flip seam-side down.
4. Place on parchment-lined baking sheet. Cover, let rise 1-2 hours.
5. Preheat oven to 450°F with Dutch oven inside.
6. Carefully transfer dough to hot Dutch oven. Cover.
7. Bake covered 30 minutes. Uncover, bake 15-20 more until deep golden.
8. Cool completely before slicing.

## Notes

- Long rise develops complex flavor.
- Dutch oven creates steam for crusty crust.
- Cool completely or crumb will be gummy.
