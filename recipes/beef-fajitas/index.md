# Beef Fajitas

Sizzling steak with charred peppers and onions

Serves: 4-6

## Ingredients

- 1½ lbs skirt steak
- 3 tbsp vegetable oil, divided
- 3 tbsp lime juice
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp cumin
- 2 bell peppers, sliced
- 1 large onion, sliced
- Flour tortillas
- Sour cream, guacamole, salsa

## Steps

1. Mix 2 tbsp oil, lime juice, garlic, cumin, salt, pepper. Marinate steak 30 min to 2 hours.
2. Cook peppers and onions in remaining oil over high until charred, 8-10 minutes. Transfer out.
3. Add steak to screaming hot pan. Sear 3-4 minutes per side for medium-rare.
4. Rest 5 minutes. Slice thin against grain.
5. Warm tortillas. Serve steak with peppers and onions.

## Notes

- Skirt steak is traditional.
- Cast iron gets hot enough for proper char.
- Don't marinate more than 2 hours.
