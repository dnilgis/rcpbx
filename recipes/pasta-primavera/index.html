Pasta Primavera

Fresh vegetables in light cream sauce

Time 35 min
Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 1 lb penne
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 yellow squash, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup asparagus, cut up
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ cup white wine
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup Parmesan
  • ¼ cup basil, torn

Steps

  1. Cook pasta. Reserve 1 cup pasta water.
  2. Sauté zucchini and squash in oil until browned, 3-4 minutes. Set aside.
  3. Add butter, sauté asparagus 2 minutes. Add garlic, cook 30 seconds.
  4. Add wine, reduce by half. Add cream and tomatoes.
  5. Return vegetables with peas. Simmer 2 minutes.
  6. Add pasta and Parmesan. Toss, adding pasta water for consistency.
  7. Stir in basil, season to taste.

Notes

Cook each vegetable separately for best doneness.

Use whatever vegetables are in season.
