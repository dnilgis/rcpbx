# Pizza Dough

Chewy, crispy, restaurant quality

Makes: 2 pizzas

## Ingredients

- 3 cups bread flour
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 2¼ tsp instant yeast
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup warm water
- 2 tbsp olive oil

## Steps

1. Mix flour, sugar, yeast, salt.
2. Add water and oil. Mix until dough forms.
3. Knead until smooth and elastic, 8-10 minutes.
4. Place in oiled bowl. Cover, let rise until doubled, 1-2 hours.
5. Punch down. Divide in half.
6. Use immediately or refrigerate up to 3 days.
7. Let cold dough come to room temp before shaping.

## Notes

- Bread flour has more gluten for chewy texture.
- Cold rise develops more flavor.
- High heat is essential for crispy crust.
