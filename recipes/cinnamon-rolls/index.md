# Cinnamon Rolls

Soft, gooey, cream cheese frosted

Serves: 12

## Ingredients

- Dough: 1 cup warm milk, 2¼ tsp yeast, ¼ cup sugar, ⅓ cup butter softened, 1 egg, 3½ cups flour, 1 tsp salt
- Filling: ⅓ cup butter softened, ¾ cup brown sugar, 2 tbsp cinnamon
- Glaze: 4 oz cream cheese, ¼ cup butter, 1 cup powdered sugar, ½ tsp vanilla

## Steps

1. Dissolve yeast in warm milk with pinch sugar. Let sit 5 minutes.
2. Mix in sugar, butter, egg. Add flour and salt. Knead until smooth, 8 minutes.
3. Let rise until doubled, 1 hour.
4. Roll to 16x12 rectangle. Spread with butter. Mix brown sugar and cinnamon, sprinkle over.
5. Roll up tightly from long side. Cut into 12 pieces.
6. Place in greased 9x13 pan. Let rise 30 minutes.
7. Bake at 350°F for 25-30 minutes.
8. Beat glaze ingredients. Spread on warm rolls.

## Notes

- Warm (not hot) milk activates yeast.
- Use dental floss to cut dough.
- Don't overbake.
