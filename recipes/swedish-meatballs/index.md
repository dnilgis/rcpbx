# Swedish Meatballs

Tender meatballs in creamy gravy. Better than IKEA.

Prep: 25 min · Cook: 25 min · Serves: 6

## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground beef
- ½ lb ground pork
- 1 egg
- ¼ cup breadcrumbs
- ¼ cup milk
- 1 tsp salt
- ¼ tsp allspice
- ¼ tsp nutmeg
- 3 tbsp butter
- 3 tbsp flour
- 2 cups beef broth
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce

## Steps

1. Soak breadcrumbs in milk for 5 minutes.
2. Mix beef, pork, egg, soaked breadcrumbs, salt, allspice, and nutmeg. Don't overmix.
3. Roll into 1-inch balls. You should get about 30.
4. Brown meatballs in batches in butter over medium heat. Set aside.
5. In same pan, whisk flour into drippings. Cook 1 minute.
6. Slowly whisk in beef broth, then cream. Simmer until thickened, about 5 minutes.
7. Add Worcestershire. Return meatballs to sauce.
8. Simmer 10 minutes until meatballs are cooked through.
9. Serve over egg noodles or mashed potatoes.

## Notes

- Allspice is key: It's the signature Swedish flavor.
- Don't skip the pork: It keeps meatballs tender.
- Make ahead: Meatballs freeze well in sauce.
