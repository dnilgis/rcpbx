# French Toast

Custardy center, golden exterior

Serves: 4

## Ingredients

- 8 slices thick brioche or challah
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup whole milk
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- ½ tsp cinnamon
- Pinch salt
- 2 tbsp butter
- Maple syrup and powdered sugar

## Steps

1. Whisk eggs, milk, sugar, vanilla, cinnamon, salt.
2. Heat griddle over medium. Add butter.
3. Dip bread in custard, 20-30 seconds per side.
4. Cook until golden, 3-4 minutes per side.
5. Add more butter between batches.
6. Keep warm in 200°F oven.
7. Serve with maple syrup and powdered sugar.

## Notes

- Thick bread works best.
- Day-old bread absorbs better.
- Don't over-soak.
