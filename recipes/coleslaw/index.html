Coleslaw

Creamy, tangy, classic BBQ side

Time15 min + chilling
Serves8

Ingredients

  • 1 head green cabbage, shredded (8 cups)
  • 2 carrots, shredded
  • ¾ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • ½ tsp celery seed
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Toss cabbage and carrots.
  2. Whisk mayo, sugar, vinegar, mustard, celery seed.
  3. Pour over cabbage. Toss to coat.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Refrigerate at least 1 hour.
  6. Toss again before serving.

Notes

Salting cabbage first draws out moisture.

Letting it sit melds flavors.
