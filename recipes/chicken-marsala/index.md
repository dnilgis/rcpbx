# Chicken Marsala

Pan-seared chicken in mushroom wine sauce

Serves: 4

## Ingredients

- 4 chicken breasts
- ½ cup flour
- 4 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 8 oz cremini mushrooms, sliced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup dry Marsala wine
- 1 cup chicken broth
- ½ cup heavy cream
- 2 tbsp parsley

## Steps

1. Pound chicken to ½-inch. Season, dredge in flour.
2. Cook chicken in 2 tbsp butter and oil until golden, 3-4 minutes per side. Transfer out.
3. Add remaining butter and mushrooms. Brown 5 minutes. Add garlic, cook 30 seconds.
4. Add Marsala, scrape up browned bits. Reduce by half.
5. Add broth, simmer 5 minutes. Stir in cream.
6. Return chicken, simmer until cooked through, 3-5 minutes. Add parsley.

## Notes

- Use dry Marsala, not sweet cooking Marsala.
- Pounding chicken ensures even cooking.
