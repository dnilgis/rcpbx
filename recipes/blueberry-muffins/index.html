Blueberry Muffins

Tender, bursting with berries

Time40 min
Serves12

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1½ cups blueberries
  • Coarse sugar for topping

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Line 12-cup muffin tin.
  2. Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, salt.
  3. Whisk oil, egg, milk, vanilla.
  4. Pour wet into dry. Stir until just combined.
  5. Fold in blueberries.
  6. Divide among cups. Sprinkle with coarse sugar.
  7. Bake 20-25 minutes until golden.

Notes

Don't overmix - lumps are fine.

Frozen blueberries work great.

Fill cups nearly full for big tops.
