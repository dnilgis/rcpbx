# Roasted Pork Tenderloin

Quick-roasting, herb-crusted, juicy

Serves: 4-6

## Ingredients

- 2 pork tenderloins (about 2 lbs)
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp fresh rosemary, minced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- ½ tsp red pepper flakes
- Salt and pepper

## Steps

1. Pat tenderloins dry.
2. Mix sugar, rosemary, garlic, pepper flakes, 1 tsp salt, ½ tsp pepper.
3. Rub with oil, then coat with spice mixture.
4. Sear in oven-safe skillet over medium-high on all sides, 8 minutes.
5. Transfer to 400°F oven. Roast until 140-145°F, 15-20 minutes.
6. Rest 10 minutes. Slice into medallions.

## Notes

- Tenderloins are lean - don't overcook.
- Sugar helps browning.
