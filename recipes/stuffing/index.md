# Stuffing

Herbed bread, savory, buttery

Serves: 10-12

## Ingredients

- 1 lb crusty bread, cubed and dried
- 6 tbsp butter
- 1 large onion, diced
- 3 celery ribs, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh sage, chopped
- 1 tbsp fresh thyme
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 2 eggs, beaten
- Salt and pepper

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Butter 9x13 dish.
2. Dry bread cubes in oven 15 minutes if not already stale.
3. Melt butter. Cook onion and celery until soft, 8 minutes.
4. Add garlic, sage, thyme. Cook 1 minute. Season.
5. Combine bread, vegetables, broth, eggs. Toss well.
6. Transfer to dish. Cover with foil.
7. Bake 30 minutes covered. Uncover, bake 20 more minutes until golden.

## Notes

- Dried bread absorbs more liquid.
- Fresh herbs make a huge difference.
