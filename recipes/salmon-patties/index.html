Salmon Patties

Old-fashioned comfort, crispy outside

Time25 min
Serves4

Ingredients

  • 1 (14.75 oz) can salmon, drained
  • ¼ cup onion, minced
  • ¼ cup cornmeal
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Lemon and tartar sauce

Steps

  1. Drain salmon, flake into bowl, remove bones and skin.
  2. Add onion, cornmeal, egg, flour. Mix well.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Form into 4-6 patties.
  5. Heat ¼ inch oil in skillet over medium.
  6. Fry until golden, 3-4 minutes per side.
  7. Drain. Serve with lemon and tartar sauce.

Notes

Canned salmon works perfectly.

Cornmeal gives authentic Southern texture.
