Baked Mac & Cheese

Creamy, cheesy, golden-crusted perfection.

Prep: 20 min Cook: 35 min Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 lb elbow macaroni
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups whole milk
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • ¼ tsp cayenne (optional)
  • 3 cups sharp cheddar, shredded (12 oz)
  • 1 cup Gruyere, shredded (4 oz)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • Topping: 1 cup panko breadcrumbs, 2 tbsp butter melted, ¼ cup parmesan grated

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Butter a 9x13 baking dish.
  2. Cook pasta 2 minutes less than package directions (it'll finish in oven). Drain.
  3. Melt butter in large pot over medium heat. Whisk in flour, cook 1 minute (this is the roux).
  4. Slowly whisk in milk. Cook, whisking constantly, until thickened, about 5 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat. Stir in mustard, cayenne, cheddar, and Gruyere until melted. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Fold in pasta. Transfer to prepared dish.
  7. Mix panko with melted butter and parmesan. Sprinkle over top.
  8. Bake 25-30 minutes until bubbly. Broil 2 minutes for extra-crispy top (watch carefully).

Notes

  • SHRED YOUR OWN CHEESE. Pre-shredded has anti-caking agents that prevent smooth melting. This is the #1 tip.
  • All cheddar works: Skip Gruyere if needed. Use 4 cups sharp cheddar total.
  • Stovetop version: Skip baking. Just serve the cheese sauce with pasta directly.
  • Storage: Keeps 4 days refrigerated. Reheat with splash of milk to restore creaminess.
