BBQ Sauce

Sweet, tangy, smoky

Time30 min
Serves2 cups

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ketchup
  • ½ cup apple cider vinegar
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp molasses
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire
  • 1 tbsp yellow mustard
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp onion powder
  • Salt and cayenne to taste

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in saucepan.
  2. Bring to simmer over medium heat.
  3. Reduce heat to low. Simmer 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Taste and adjust sugar, vinegar, and heat.
  5. Use immediately or refrigerate up to 1 month.

Notes

Balance sweet, tangy, and smoky to your taste.

Thickens as it cools.

Add chipotle for smoky heat.
