# Lemon Bars

Buttery shortbread crust, tangy lemon filling, powdered sugar snow. Perfect.

Prep: 20 min · Cook: 45 min · Makes: 16 bars

## Ingredients

**For the crust:**
- 1 cup (2 sticks) butter, softened
- 1/2 cup powdered sugar
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/4 tsp salt
**For the filling:**
- 4 large eggs
- 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
- 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
- 2/3 cup fresh lemon juice (about 4 lemons)
- Zest of 2 lemons
- Powdered sugar for dusting

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a 9x13 inch pan with parchment, leaving overhang for easy removal.
2. Make the crust: Beat butter and powdered sugar until fluffy. Add flour and salt, mix until crumbly. Press evenly into pan.
3. Bake crust 18-20 minutes until edges are light golden. Don't overbake—it continues cooking with the filling.
4. Make the filling: Whisk eggs and sugar until smooth. Add flour, lemon juice, and zest. Whisk until combined.
5. Pour filling over hot crust. Return to oven and bake 22-25 minutes until filling is set and no longer jiggles.
6. Cool completely in pan. Refrigerate at least 2 hours.
7. Lift out using parchment. Dust generously with powdered sugar. Cut into bars.

## Notes

- Fresh lemons only: Bottled juice doesn't compare. You need that bright, fresh flavor.
- Hot crust: Pour filling onto hot crust to help it set properly.
- Clean cuts: Wipe knife between cuts. Use a thin, sharp blade.
- Storage: Refrigerate up to 5 days. Dust with powdered sugar just before serving.
- Room temp: Let sit 10 minutes before serving for best texture.
