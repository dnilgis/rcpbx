# Broccoli Cheddar Soup

Silky, cheesy, actually tastes like broccoli

Serves: 6-8

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp unsalted butter
- 2 lbs broccoli, florets chopped, stems sliced thin
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1½ tsp dry mustard
- Pinch cayenne
- 1 tsp salt
- ¼ tsp baking soda
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 3-4 cups water
- 2 oz baby spinach
- 3 oz sharp cheddar, shredded
- 1½ oz Parmesan, grated

## Steps

1. Melt butter in Dutch oven over medium-high. Add broccoli, onion, garlic, mustard, cayenne, and salt. Cook 6 minutes.
2. Add 1 cup water and baking soda. Cover, simmer 20 minutes until broccoli is very soft.
3. Add broth and 2 cups water. When simmering, add spinach, cook 1 minute.
4. Blend in batches with cheeses until smooth.
5. Return to pot, simmer. Adjust consistency with water. Season to taste.

## Notes

- Baking soda speeds breakdown for sweeter flavor.
- Overcooking broccoli is actually the key here.
- Don't boil after adding cheese or it separates.
