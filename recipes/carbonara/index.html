Spaghetti Carbonara

Silky egg sauce, crispy pork, no cream

Time 25 min
Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 8 oz guanciale or pancetta, diced
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 1½ cups Pecorino Romano, grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in well-salted water. Reserve 1½ cups pasta water.
  2. Cook guanciale in cold skillet over medium until crispy, 8-10 minutes. Remove from heat.
  3. Whisk yolks, eggs, most Pecorino, and plenty of black pepper.
  4. Drain pasta, add to skillet with guanciale (off heat).
  5. Pour egg mixture over pasta, toss vigorously. Residual heat cooks eggs.
  6. Add pasta water until sauce is silky.
  7. Serve with remaining Pecorino and pepper.

Notes

Key: take pan OFF heat before adding eggs.

Work fast - pasta must be hot.

No cream in real carbonara - ever.
