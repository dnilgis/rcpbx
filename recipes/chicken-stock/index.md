# Chicken Stock

Rich, golden, foundation of flavor

Makes: 3-4 quarts

## Ingredients

- 4-5 lbs chicken backs, wings, or carcasses
- 1 large onion, quartered
- 2 carrots, chunked
- 2 celery ribs, chunked
- 1 head garlic, halved
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 tsp black peppercorns
- Fresh thyme and parsley stems

## Steps

1. Place chicken parts in large stockpot. Cover with cold water by 2 inches.
2. Bring to boil, reduce to bare simmer. Skim any foam.
3. Add vegetables, garlic, and aromatics.
4. Simmer uncovered 4-6 hours, adding water as needed.
5. Strain through fine-mesh strainer. Discard solids.
6. Cool uncovered. Refrigerate.
7. Skim solidified fat before using.

## Notes

- Never boil - causes cloudy stock.
- More gelatin = richer body.
- Freezes for 6 months.
