# Snickerdoodles

Soft, chewy, and rolled in cinnamon sugar. The cookie that hugs you back.

Prep: 15 min · Cook: 10 min · Makes: 24 cookies

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup (1 stick) butter, softened
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 large egg
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp cream of tartar
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/4 tsp salt
**For rolling:**
- 3 Tbsp sugar
- 1 Tbsp cinnamon

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Line baking sheets with parchment.
2. Beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy, about 2 minutes.
3. Add egg and vanilla. Beat until combined.
4. In another bowl, whisk flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt.
5. Add dry ingredients to wet. Mix until just combined.
6. Mix cinnamon and sugar in a small bowl.
7. Roll dough into 1-inch balls. Roll each ball in cinnamon sugar.
8. Place 2 inches apart on baking sheets. Bake 8-10 minutes until edges are set but centers are soft.
9. Cool on pan 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.

## Notes

- Cream of tartar: Don't skip it—it gives snickerdoodles their signature tangy flavor and chewy texture.
- Soft centers: Pull them when edges are just set. They firm up as they cool.
- Crinkle tops: The cookies should crack and crinkle. That's the sign of a good snickerdoodle.
- Chill the dough: For thicker cookies, refrigerate dough 30 minutes before rolling.
- Storage: Keep in an airtight container up to 1 week.
