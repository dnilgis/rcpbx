Quiche Lorraine

Bacon, cheese, custardy filling

Time1½ hrs
Serves6-8

Ingredients

  • 1 (9-inch) pie crust, blind-baked
  • 6 slices bacon, chopped
  • 1 cup Gruyère, shredded
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ¼ tsp nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Cook bacon until crisp. Drain.
  3. Scatter bacon and cheese in pie crust.
  4. Whisk eggs, cream, milk, nutmeg, ½ tsp salt, ¼ tsp pepper.
  5. Pour custard into crust.
  6. Bake until center is just set and jiggles slightly, 45-50 minutes.
  7. Cool 15 minutes before slicing.

Notes

Blind-baking prevents soggy crust.

Custard should jiggle like jello when done.

Don't overbake.
