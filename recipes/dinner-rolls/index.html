Dinner Rolls

Soft, buttery, pull-apart

Time2½ hrs
Serves16

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2¼ tsp active dry yeast
  • ⅓ cup sugar
  • ⅓ cup butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp butter, melted (for brushing)

Steps

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm milk with pinch sugar. Let sit 5 minutes.
  2. Add sugar, butter, eggs. Mix well.
  3. Add flour and salt. Knead until smooth and elastic, 8-10 minutes.
  4. Place in greased bowl. Cover, let rise until doubled, 1 hour.
  5. Punch down. Divide into 16 pieces. Roll into balls.
  6. Place in greased 9x13 pan. Cover, let rise 30 minutes.
  7. Bake at 375°F until golden, 20-25 minutes.
  8. Brush with melted butter immediately.

Notes

Dough should be slightly tacky.

Butter while hot so it soaks in.
