# Carrot Cake

Moist, spiced, cream cheese frosted

Serves: 12

## Ingredients

- Cake: 2 cups flour, 2 tsp baking soda, 2 tsp cinnamon, 1 tsp salt, 1½ cups vegetable oil, 1½ cups sugar, 4 eggs, 3 cups finely grated carrots, 1 cup walnuts
- Frosting: 16 oz cream cheese, ½ cup butter, 4 cups powdered sugar, 2 tsp vanilla

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease two 9-inch pans.
2. Whisk flour, baking soda, cinnamon, salt.
3. Beat oil, sugar, eggs. Stir in dry ingredients.
4. Fold in carrots and walnuts.
5. Divide between pans. Bake 30-35 minutes.
6. Cool 10 minutes, turn out onto racks.
7. Frosting: Beat cream cheese and butter. Add sugar and vanilla.
8. Frost cooled layers.

## Notes

- Finely grated carrots distribute better.
- Oil keeps cake moist.
- Toast walnuts for better flavor.
