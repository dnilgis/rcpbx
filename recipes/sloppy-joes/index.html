Sloppy Joes

Sweet, tangy, messy perfection

Time35 min
Serves6

Ingredients

  • 1½ lbs ground beef
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion, diced fine
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced fine
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire
  • 1 tbsp yellow mustard
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • ½ cup water
  • Hamburger buns

Steps

  1. Cook onion and pepper in oil until soft, 5 minutes.
  2. Add beef, break into small pieces. Brown 8 minutes. Drain fat.
  3. Add garlic, 30 seconds.
  4. Stir in ketchup, sugar, Worcestershire, mustard, chili powder, water.
  5. Simmer until thick, 10-15 minutes. Season to taste.
  6. Toast buns. Pile on the sloppy joe.

Notes

Break meat small for classic texture.

Let simmer until thick.

Vinegar at end adds brightness.
