Apple Pie

Flaky crust, spiced fruit filling

Prep: Cook: Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • Crust: 2½ cups flour, 1 tbsp sugar, 1 tsp salt, 1 cup cold butter cubed, ½ cup ice water
  • Filling: 3 lbs mixed apples, ¾ cup sugar, 2 tbsp flour, 1 tsp cinnamon, ¼ tsp nutmeg, 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 egg + 1 tbsp water for egg wash

Instructions

  1. Crust: Pulse flour, sugar, salt. Add butter, pulse to pea-sized. Drizzle ice water, pulse until dough forms. Divide, flatten, chill 1 hour.
  2. Peel, core, slice apples. Toss with sugar, flour, spices.
  3. Roll one disk to 12 inches. Fit into pie plate.
  4. Add filling. Dot with butter.
  5. Roll second disk. Place over filling. Trim, crimp edges. Cut vents.
  6. Brush with egg wash. Chill 30 minutes.
  7. Bake at 425°F for 20 minutes. Reduce to 375°F, bake 40-50 more until golden and bubbling.
  8. Cool 2 hours before slicing.

Notes

  • Mix apple varieties for balance.
  • Cold butter = flaky crust.
  • Must cool or filling will run.
