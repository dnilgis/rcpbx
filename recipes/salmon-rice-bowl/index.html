Salmon Rice Bowl

The TikTok-viral crispy rice bowl with everything sauce.

Prep: 10 min Cook: 15 min Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets (6 oz each), skin-on
  • 2 cups cooked sushi rice, warm
  • 1/2 cup kimchi, chopped
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 2 sheets nori, cut into strips
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Sesame seeds
  • 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • For the sauce:
  • 3 Tbsp Kewpie mayo
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sriracha
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix all sauce ingredients in a small bowl. Set aside.
  2. Pat salmon dry with paper towels. Season skin side with salt. Season flesh side with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat oil in a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Place salmon skin-side down. Press gently with spatula for first 30 seconds to prevent curling.
  4. Cook 4-5 minutes until skin is very crispy. Flip and cook 2-3 more minutes for medium (or longer if you prefer well-done).
  5. Divide rice between two bowls. Top with kimchi, avocado slices, and nori strips.
  6. Place salmon on top (skin-side up for maximum crunch). Drizzle generously with sauce.
  7. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds. Serve immediately.

Notes

  • The crispy skin is essential. Make sure salmon is very dry and pan is hot.
  • Kewpie mayo is creamier than regular mayo - find it in the Asian section.
  • Add a jammy egg (6-min boil) for extra protein.
