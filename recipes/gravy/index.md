# Gravy

Silky, savory, essential for meat

Makes: 3 cups

## Ingredients

- 4 tbsp butter (or pan drippings)
- ¼ cup flour
- 3 cups chicken or turkey stock
- Pan drippings (optional)
- Fresh thyme
- Salt and pepper

## Steps

1. Melt butter in saucepan over medium heat.
2. Whisk in flour. Cook, whisking constantly, until light brown, 2-3 minutes.
3. Gradually whisk in stock, scraping up any browned bits.
4. Add thyme. Simmer, whisking occasionally, until thickened, 10-15 minutes.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
6. Strain if desired.
7. Add any pan drippings from roasted meat.

## Notes

- Cooking flour removes raw taste.
- Gradual addition prevents lumps.
- Strain for silky texture.
