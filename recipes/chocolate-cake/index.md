# Chocolate Cake

Rich, moist, deeply chocolatey

Serves: 12

## Ingredients

- Cake: 2 cups flour, 2 cups sugar, ¾ cup cocoa powder, 2 tsp baking soda, 1 tsp salt, 1 cup vegetable oil, 1 cup buttermilk, 2 eggs, 1 cup hot coffee
- Frosting: 1 cup butter, 4 cups powdered sugar, ½ cup cocoa, ¼ cup heavy cream, 2 tsp vanilla

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease two 9-inch pans.
2. Whisk flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, salt.
3. Add oil, buttermilk, eggs. Beat 2 minutes.
4. Stir in hot coffee (batter will be thin).
5. Divide between pans. Bake 30-35 minutes.
6. Cool 10 minutes, turn out onto racks.
7. Frosting: Beat butter until fluffy. Add sugar, cocoa, cream, vanilla.
8. Frost cooled layers.

## Notes

- Hot coffee intensifies chocolate flavor.
- Batter is very thin - that's correct.
