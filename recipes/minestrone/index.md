# Minestrone

Hearty Italian vegetable soup

Serves: 8-10

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 oz pancetta, diced
- 2 onions, diced
- 2 celery ribs, diced
- 1 carrot, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 zucchini, diced
- 1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes
- 8 cups chicken broth
- 1 Parmesan rind (optional)
- 1 (15 oz) can cannellini beans
- 1 cup small pasta (ditalini)
- 2 cups chopped kale
- ¼ cup fresh basil

## Steps

1. Cook pancetta in oil until rendered, 3-4 minutes.
2. Add onions, celery, carrot. Cook 8 minutes until softened.
3. Add garlic, cook 30 seconds. Add zucchini, cook 2 minutes.
4. Add tomatoes, broth, Parmesan rind. Simmer 30 minutes.
5. Add beans and pasta. Cook until pasta is al dente, 10 minutes.
6. Add kale, cook 2 minutes. Remove rind, stir in basil. Season to taste.

## Notes

- Parmesan rind adds incredible umami depth.
- Add pasta near end so it doesn't get mushy.
- Even better the next day.
