Hash Browns

Shredded, golden, shatteringly crispy

Time35 min
Serves4

Ingredients

  • 1½ lbs russet potatoes
  • 3 tbsp butter or bacon fat
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Peel potatoes. Shred on box grater.
  2. Rinse until water runs clear. Drain well.
  3. Wrap in towel and squeeze out all moisture.
  4. Heat butter in cast iron over medium.
  5. Add potatoes in even layer. Season. Press flat.
  6. Cook without stirring until bottom is golden and crispy, 8-10 minutes.
  7. Flip in sections. Cook until second side is crispy, 8-10 minutes.

Notes

Rinsing removes starch for crispiness.

Dry, dry, dry.

Don't touch them while cooking.
