Basic Vinaigrette

Master this ratio and never buy bottled dressing again.

Prep 5 min
Cook 0 min
Serves 8

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: honey, shallot, herbs

Steps

  1. Whisk vinegar, mustard, garlic, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Slowly drizzle in olive oil while whisking constantly.
  3. Taste and adjust seasoning.
  4. Use immediately or store refrigerated up to 1 week.

Notes

The ratio: 3 parts oil to 1 part acid. Adjust to taste.

Mustard is the emulsifier: It helps oil and vinegar stay mixed.

Variations: Swap vinegar for lemon juice. Add honey for sweetness. Use shallot instead of garlic.
