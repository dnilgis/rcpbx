Béchamel

Creamy white sauce, building block

Time15 min
Serves2 cups

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 cups whole milk, warmed
  • ¼ tsp nutmeg
  • Salt and white pepper

Steps

  1. Melt butter in saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Whisk in flour. Cook 1 minute without browning.
  3. Gradually whisk in warm milk.
  4. Simmer, whisking frequently, until thick enough to coat a spoon, 5-7 minutes.
  5. Season with nutmeg, salt, and white pepper.
  6. Use immediately or press plastic wrap directly on surface to prevent skin.

Notes

Warm milk prevents lumps.

Don't brown the flour - should stay white.

Base for cheese sauce, gratins, lasagna.
