Fish Tacos

Spiced fish, tangy crema, fresh slaw

Time45 min
Serves4-6

Ingredients

  • 1½ lbs firm white fish (mahi, cod, tilapia)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp hot sauce
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • Corn tortillas
  • Cilantro, lime, salsa

Steps

  1. Mix mayo, lime juice, hot sauce for crema.
  2. Mix chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, 1 tsp salt.
  3. Pat fish dry. Cut into strips. Toss with oil and spices.
  4. Cook fish over high heat 2-3 minutes per side until just cooked.
  5. Warm tortillas.
  6. Serve fish in tortillas with cabbage, crema, cilantro, lime.

Notes

Mahi or cod hold up best.

Don't overcook - fish should just flake.
