# Biscuits and Gravy

Creamy sausage gravy over warm biscuits

Serves: 4-6

## Ingredients

- 1 lb breakfast sausage
- 3 tbsp flour
- 2½ cups whole milk
- ½ tsp black pepper
- Pinch cayenne
- Salt
- 4-6 fresh biscuits

## Steps

1. Cook sausage over medium, breaking into small pieces, until browned, 8-10 minutes.
2. Sprinkle flour over sausage. Stir, cook 1 minute.
3. Gradually whisk in milk, scraping up browned bits.
4. Simmer, stirring, until thickened, 5-7 minutes.
5. Season with pepper, cayenne, salt to taste.
6. Split warm biscuits. Ladle gravy over top.

## Notes

- Don't drain sausage fat - it flavors gravy.
- Gravy thickens as it cools.
- Black pepper is essential.
