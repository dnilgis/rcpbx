# Split Pea Soup

Thick, smoky, and deeply satisfying

Serves: 6-8

## Ingredients

- 1 smoked ham hock
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 onions, chopped
- 2 celery ribs, chopped
- 2 carrots, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 lb green split peas, rinsed
- 8 cups water
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 tsp fresh thyme
- Salt and pepper

## Steps

1. Melt butter. Cook onions, celery, carrots until softened, 8-10 minutes.
2. Add garlic, cook 30 seconds.
3. Add peas, water, ham hock, bay leaves, thyme. Bring to boil.
4. Reduce heat, simmer partially covered until thick, about 1 hour.
5. Remove ham hock, shred meat, return to soup. Discard bone.
6. Remove bay leaves. Season with salt and pepper.

## Notes

- No need to soak split peas.
- Soup thickens significantly as it cools - thin when reheating.
