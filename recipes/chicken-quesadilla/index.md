# Chicken Quesadilla

Crispy tortilla, melty cheese, seasoned chicken. Ready in 15 minutes.

Prep: 10 min · Cook: 10 min · Serves: 4

## Ingredients

- 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 4 large flour tortillas
- 2 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend
- 1/2 cup bell peppers, diced
- 1/4 cup red onion, diced
- 2 Tbsp butter or oil
- Sour cream, salsa, guacamole for serving

## Steps

1. Toss shredded chicken with chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, and salt.
2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add 1/2 Tbsp butter.
3. Place a tortilla in the skillet. Add 1/2 cup cheese to half the tortilla.
4. Top cheese with 1/4 of the chicken, peppers, and onion. Add more cheese on top.
5. Fold tortilla in half. Cook 2-3 minutes until bottom is golden.
6. Flip carefully and cook 2-3 minutes more until both sides are crispy and cheese is melted.
7. Repeat with remaining ingredients. Cut into triangles and serve with toppings.

## Notes

- Crispy secret: Butter in the pan creates the best golden crust.
- Cheese placement: Cheese on both sides of the filling helps everything stick together.
- Rotisserie shortcut: Use store-bought rotisserie chicken for speed.
- Make it veggie: Swap chicken for black beans and corn.
- Keep warm: Place finished quesadillas on a baking sheet in a 200°F oven while making the rest.
